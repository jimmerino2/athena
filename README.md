
# Athena
Athena is an intelligent career guidance tool designed to help you discover and pursue your ideal career path. Powered by Google's Gemini, Athena offers a seamless and interactive experience to explore careers, prepare for the job market, and stay ahead of industry trends.


## [Project Documentation](https://github.com/jimmerino2/athena/blob/develop/Documentation.md)

## Features
 - **Career Discovery Quiz**: Take a quick and insightful quiz to uncover your ideal job based on your interests, skills, and goals.
 - **Course Recommendations**: Get personalized learning resources and course suggestions tailored to your ideal career.
 - **Local Job Listings**: Find real-time job openings near you that match your ideal role.
 - **Mock Interview Simulator**: Simulate interviews for specific jobs and get feedback to improve your responses.
 - **Resume Rating**: Upload your resume and get constructive feedback with a rating and suggestions to optimize it.

## Powered By
-   [**Gemini**](https://gemini.google.com/) - For smart course and job recommendations, interview simulation, and resume analysis
-   [**Flutter**](https://flutter.dev/) - For a beautiful, fast, and cross-platform user interface
-   [**Firebase**](https://firebase.google.com/) - For real-time data handling, authentication, and backend services


## Installation
 1. [Setup flutter on your device](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install)
 2. Clone the repository:
	```
	git clone https://github.com/jimmerino2/athena.git
	cd athena
	```
3. Install dependencies:
`flutter pub get`

4. Run the app:
`flutter run`
